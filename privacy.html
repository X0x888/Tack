Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

Tack ("the App") is a macOS application developed by the Tack team ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Tack does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data. The App operates entirely offline on your device.

Specifically, Tack does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, or any identifiers)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks
  • Transmit any data over the internet
  • Require user accounts or authentication
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

2. Data Storage

All content you create in Tack — including notes, folders, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage frameworks. This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it and cannot retrieve it.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is removed with it.

3. Third-Party Services

Tack does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, advertising networks, or cloud storage platforms. The App contains no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

The App uses open-source libraries (MarkdownUI and HighlightSwift) solely for rendering Markdown content and syntax highlighting. These libraries operate entirely on-device and do not transmit any data.

4. Export Feature

Tack allows you to export your notes as Markdown (.md) or plain text (.txt) files to a location you choose on your device. This export is initiated solely by you and does not involve any network transmission. The exported files remain under your complete control.

5. Children's Privacy

Because Tack does not collect any data from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations by design — no data is collected.

6. European Users (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights regarding personal data. Since Tack does not collect, process, or store any personal data, these rights are satisfied by default. There is no personal data to access, rectify, erase, or port.

7. California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have specific rights regarding personal information. Since Tack does not collect, sell, or share any personal information, these rights are inherently fulfilled. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this policy.
